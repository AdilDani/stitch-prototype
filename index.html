Stitch prototype // V 0.1.0 — internal review // 20 pages · linked HTML · no backend
A platform for serialized immersive fiction
STITCH
A reader spends their evening inside a novel-themed enclosure. An author composes in paragraphs, scores them with normal-distribution audio envelopes, and publishes to a spoiler-aware forum. This is the prototype shell.
